Our Mission

We believe that when basic needs are met, people can focus on learning, growing, and building a future rooted in stability, dignity, and hope.

The Rooted Hands INC. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to nurturing individuals and families by meeting essential needs — like food, school supplies, and sports equipment — while also creating pathways for long-term growth through access to education and scholarships.

Founded in New York and serving communities locally and in the Caribbean, we operate with the conviction that care is the foundation of opportunity. Every backpack, every meal, every scholarship is a seed planted for a stronger tomorrow.

"Nurture. Connect. Grow. These are not just words on our logo — they are the principles that guide every decision we make."
What We Do

Our Programs

Four pillars of support designed to meet immediate needs and create lasting change.

🎒

School Supplies & Backpacks

We assemble and distribute complete school supply kits — backpacks, notebooks, pencils, crayons, and more — to ensure every child starts the school year prepared and confident.

🍎

Food Distribution

Through community partnerships and direct outreach, we provide nutritious food to families experiencing food insecurity, both locally in New York and internationally in the Caribbean.

🏆

Sports Equipment

We supply youth athletic programs and individual athletes with essential sports equipment, promoting physical health, teamwork, and personal development through athletics.

🎓

Educational Scholarships

Our need-based scholarships help youth pursue primary, secondary, and post-secondary education. Funds are paid directly to institutions, removing financial barriers to academic success.

🌍

International Outreach

We partner with local organizations in Caribbean countries to deliver food, supplies, and educational resources, extending our mission beyond borders with documented, accountable shipments.

🤝

Community Engagement

Through drives, events, and volunteer coordination, we build awareness and mobilize community members to join us in creating meaningful, measurable impact.
