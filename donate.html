Rooted in Care. Growing Through Opportunity.

The Rooted Hands INC. is a New York-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to nurturing individuals and families by meeting essential needs — food, school supplies, and sports equipment — while creating pathways for long-term growth through education and scholarships.

Founded by Dawin Liranzo Galan and guided by a volunteer board, we serve communities locally and across the Caribbean with documented, accountable, and compassionate support.

"We believe that when basic needs are met, people can focus on learning, growing, and building a future rooted in stability, dignity, and hope."
🌱

Common Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. The Rooted Hands INC. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. You will receive an email receipt for tax purposes.

Where does my money go?

100% of donations fund our programs — school supplies, food distribution, sports equipment, and scholarships. We are an all-volunteer organization with no paid staff.

Can I donate supplies instead of money?

Absolutely. We accept new and gently used backpacks, school supplies, non-perishable food, and sports equipment. Contact us at info@therootedhands.org to arrange a drop-off.

How do I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers for packing events, distribution days, and administrative support. Follow us on Instagram @therootedhands for upcoming volunteer opportunities.
